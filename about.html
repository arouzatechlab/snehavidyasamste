About SNEHA VIDYA SAMSTE

More than two decades of educational excellence, values, discipline and community service dedicated to shaping future generations.

Who We Are

A School Built on Service, Values and Educational Excellence

SNEHA VIDYA SAMSTE was established in the year 1999 at Bharathi Nagar under the visionary leadership of Shri Dasegowda. Founded as a service-oriented educational organization, our primary mission has always been to make premier, value-based education highly accessible to rural families. Over the last 25 years, our commitment to nurturing academic brilliance, moral integrity, and social duty has transformed SNEHA VIDYA SAMSTE into a respected citadel of learning.

Our Vision

To empower every child with knowledge, core ethics, self-confidence, creativity, and leadership competencies that prepare them to confidently adapt to a modern globalized world while staying strongly rooted in human service.

Our Mission

To deliver affordable, top-tier, quality education that integrates innovation, stringent mental discipline, comprehensive life skills, and social duty to foster highly responsible global citizens.

Message From The President

Leading Through Service and Vision
