Admission Guidelines

Please review our step-by-step admission guidelines and procedural policy framework carefully.

01

Every parent shall visit and fill the enquiry form first. It includes details of the child and the parents. Parents are advised not to fill more than one enquiry form for one applicant.

02

Admission is on First Come First Serve Basis, ensuring a transparent and sequential allocation mechanism for applicants.

03

Admission to Grades Pre KG and KG will be through a formal, child-friendly interaction of the parents and child with our lead teacher.

04

Admission to Grades I and above will be formally based on the comprehensive written entrance syllabus test.

05

Date and exact time slot for the parent interaction or candidate entrance test will be timely intimated directly to the parents.

06

The final decision of granting student admission will be reserved solely by the School authority based on the Test/Interaction results.

07

The Registration fee can be submitted by the parents before interaction/entrance test. Kindly note that registration charge is non-refundable and needs to be paid one time only.

08

Incomplete registration forms or profiles lacking required authentic declarations will strictly not be accepted or processed.

09

Kindly submit all below mentioned documents along with remaining amount within 15 days of submitting registration charges, failing which the offer of admission stands cancelled and the seat will be offered to another applicant.

Documents Required at the Time of Admission

Ensure you hold clear copies of the following digital and physical verifications during registration validation.

Birth Certificate
Child's birth record attested explicitly by parents.
Residence Proof
Aadhaar Card, Passport, Electricity Bill, Ration Card, or Voter ID.
Government Issued ID
Aadhaar Card of the child and both parents.
Leaving Certificate
Required for Grade II and upwards from the previous institution.
Passport Photos
6 photos of the child and 2 photos of both parents.
Blood Group Report
Authorized lab certificate verifying child's blood type.
Bank Passbook
Passbook details or copy owned under child's profile.
